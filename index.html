San Francisco Crime Analysis

This portfolio presents three complementary visualizations exploring crime patterns in San Francisco. Together they highlight time-based behavior, district-level differences, and the geographic concentration of incidents.

Sunday Crime Patterns: Interactive View

An interactive exploration focused on Sunday incidents, designed to reveal how offense categories and frequencies shift throughout the day.

Crime Density Heatmap Across the City

A spatial heatmap showing where crime reports cluster, helping identify high-intensity areas and neighborhood-level concentration patterns.

Crime Ratios by District

A district-level comparison chart that summarizes how crime composition differs across policing districts, supporting cross-area interpretation and prioritization.
